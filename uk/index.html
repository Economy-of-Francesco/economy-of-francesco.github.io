Economy of Francesco UK Hub

The Economy of Francesco (EoF) UK Hub connects people and projects across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland, uniting those committed to building an economy that serves everyone and leaves no one behind.

It is part of the global network of EoF hubs working to shape a more inclusive and sustainable economy.

Our mission is to amplify the voices shaping a fairer, more inclusive economy across the UK. The EoF UK Hub is a space for communities and groups who are leading local projects, hosting events, or developing grassroots solutions that re-imagines how our economy works.
