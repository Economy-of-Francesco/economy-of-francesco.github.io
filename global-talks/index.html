Creating a culture of peace and fostering a better world for our children to live — Jean Fabre (9 August 2021)

Have you ever wondered how can we work towards a culture of peace, to create a world where children and future generations can thrive and flourish?

The Economy of Francesco's thematic village Policies and happiness organised a dialogue and training session together with Jean Fabre, United Nations Consultant, Former Deputy-Director of the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) office in Geneva; Founder of the World Alliance of Cities Against Poverty.
